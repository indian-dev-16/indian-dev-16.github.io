Guidelines for SANGHARSH


1. BREAK DOWN: In this event the participants need to build the suspension bridge from the given material. The length of the bridge will be given to them. They need to construct that bridge of a given length in a given time period. After the bridge is constructed the appropriate static loads will be applied on it. The bridge which will bear maximum load will win the game.

2. SUPER STRUCTURE: In this event the participants will be given a definite area in which they have to construct a structure/building through the given blocks. The blocks will be limited and equally distributed to each and every participant. The participant will have to build a structure in a given period of time. The structure having maximum height compared to the other contenders will win.

3. SEISM-O-CURE : In this event the participants need to make a structure of given blocks of specific weight. The structure should have base isolation technique in it. Then structure will be put on a shaker machine which will shake the structure to a given frequency. The structure which will resist the max frequency will win.

4. CIRCUIT DESIGN: The main focus of this event is to detect the bugs in the circuit, to pinpoint the blunder in the circuit and to enhance the debugging capability.

5. ELECTRICAL 3 MIN: This is an electrical elocution competition. A technical term related to the E.C department. or Electrical Department will be given to the students and they will have to justify that term with respect to their department.

6. MICROTONICS :This is an asset for the electronics and communication department. In this event the students are required to make a circuit with the help of different micro controllers and interface it with its software.

7. CHEMO-CAR: This is an exclusive event of the chemical department. This event mainly focuses on the knowledge of the students in different types of chemical and their mixtures as well as their reactions. In this event the students are supposed to make a mixture of chemical which they have to use it to run a car as far as possible.

NOTE: The quantity of the mixture is same for all the participants. 8. TECHNOTHON: It is the 9 hour continuous coding competition in which students will be assigned a certain amount of problems which is to be implemented in any programming language.

9. BUILD C-NATION: Build C Nation is a ‘C’ coding completion divided into multiple rounds where each team have to solve problems related to C to jump into next round. Final round will be the Programming round.

10. LOGO DESIGNING: Participants have to design a Logo for the provided theme or the institute with the help of the Photoshop designing tool.

11. .apk BUILDER : Participants have to build an application in Android as per provided template.

12. CAD WAR: World of designing is running more than the speed of light Now it’s time to express your imagination power. Here “CAD WAR” is challenging your designing brilliance. Now the battle has begun so challenge your mind. If you are mad for cad so get armed up with all your design weapons and tricks in this war of designing.

13. AUTOMANIA: In this event, there will be 2 phases: phase 1 consists of a written test in which a certain no. of students scoring highest will be promoted to phase-2. Further phase-2 will have five rounds of quiz related to automobile branch.

14. ASSEMBLER: Participants will be given electronic, electrical or mechanical devices. They are required to assemble the components.

Guidelines for KURUKSHETRA


1. HELL IN CELL - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable of fighting the one on one tournament.

2. TUG OF WAR - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable to pull off the opponent towards its side.

3. MISSION IMPOSSIBLE - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable to complete the desired path having many obstacles in a minimum time.

4. ROBO SNOOKER - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable of putting the ball in the pocket by hitting it with a cue ball.

5. CATCH ME IF YOU CAN - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable to reach first at the finishing line.

6. FIFA’16 - Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable of playing soccer in the one on one tournament.

Guidelines for SRIJAN


1. OK GOOGLE: The participant needs to write efficient queries on Google to search particular thing within less time.

2. PHOTOGRAPHY: The registered participants have to take the photographs of the events taking place in the Talaash techfest. At the evening, each participant will submit their 3 best photographs to the coordinators of the event.

3. COLLAGE MAKING: In this event, students will be provided with different themes on the spot to prepare collage. They will have to come with the materials which are required to make the collage. The A1 sheet will be given to them on the day of competition.

4. 3-D PAINTING: In this event students will be provided with an A1 sheet, they have to come with the materials which are required to paint 3D image of their own choice.

5. LIFT-OFF: This event is about how you put in all your brain cells to come up with an innovative water propelled rocket which attempts to defy the laws of gravity and remain airborne for the maximum duration. This will test the endurance of the rocket. There will be 2 criteria to judge the event: 1) The range of flight 2) The time of flight

6. JUNKYARD: This event is about how creative and smart one can be at the same time and can solve the given problem. Participants will be given random scrap material (the only inventory) out of which they have to build a working model (machine) which can perform the specified task. It's a test of one’s dexterity and intuitive thought process.

7. SM2 : This event is about social media marketing in which the participants need to create a Facebook page on the topic given to them. The participant will have to post on the Facebook page and the participant with the highest post reach will be the SM2 ninja.

Guidelines for PRADARSHANI


1. EXHIBITION: The participants will showcase their prepared models in a specified area. Out of those models the best one will be awarded.

2. PAPER PRESENTATION: The event aims at giving a platform to the young engineers to fuel their brains to ideate the ideas that never existed. The participants are supposed to write their thoughts about a particular topic concerning any field (e.g. civil, mechanical, computer, chemical, electrical, general, etc.) on a piece of paper.

3. POSTER PRESENTATION : The participants will be drawing a poster on an A2 size sheet on the given topic. Later on they will be giving presentation on the same topic.

4. JUST IN 2 MINUTES: In this event the participant needs to explain the case study on a topic (related to CIVIL ENGINEERING) given to them few days before the event. The participant will have to present the whole topic in 2 minutes through poster presentation. NOTE: Decision to be taken by judge panel.

5. SMART THEME CITY: In this event the participants need to design a theme city. The theme will be given to them. They have to plan the city and are supposed to make the model/prototype in the given period of time. The best model made will win the competition. Decision to be taken by judges.

Guidelines for ABHIVYAKTI


1. DEBATE: The participants will debate on the topic given to them in the predefined period of time. Note: Topic will be given to the participant few days before the competition.

2. EXTEMPORE: The participants will be given an ‘on the spot’ topic on which they are supposed to express their views in a given period of time.

2. FICTITIOUS RECRUITMENT: In this event the participant will be undergoing a fake recruitment process consisting of 3 rounds i.e. aptitude test followed by group discussion and personal interview.

3. CHEMO – QUIZ: It is a simple quiz only for the students of the chemical dept. It is a simple quiz based on the questionnaire on different subject in chemical engineering. Along with an anchor and participants.

4. LONE SURVIVOR: It is an event consisting of following group of events:
Talaash the Teknoloji : Logo Identification
Technical Quiz
Techno Crossword

6. TREASURE HUNT: In this event participants have to form a team of 3 and have to chase clues provided by us. The team with highest points will be declared winner and not the one who completes the hunt in the first place.

Guidelines for KHEL KHEL MEIN


1. ANGRY BIRD: We will have real game booth in our campus where the person can play angry birds! The unique, full-body experience also demonstrates the physics behind the Angry Birds game, providing an integrated approach to learning. Visitors will experience educational concepts in the game in an energetic and an exciting environment.

2. CANDY CRUSH: It is a simple fun gaming just like the mobile gaming. In this the participants have to play the game in real life size candy crush arena. This event will give joy to the participants and also test their brain ability to solve problems.

3. SNAKES AND LADDER: Snakes and Ladders will be a board game played by candidates. Number displayed on the dice will allow the player to move forward and will get closer to the finishing point.

4. LASER TAG: It is a team or an individual sport or a recreational activity where players attempt to score points by tagging targets, typically with a hand-held laser emitting targeting device. Infrared-sensitive targets are commonly worn by each player and are sometimes integrated within the arena in which the game is played.

5. COUNTER STRIKE: Counter-Strike is a first-person shooter game in which players join either the terrorist team or the counter-terrorist team or become a spectator. Each team attempts to complete their mission objective and/or eliminate the opposing team. Each round starts with the two teams spawning simultaneously.

6. NEED FOR SPEED: Need for Speed is a racing video game where the main objective is to win the race in a variety of game mode eluding traffic and the police.

7. IMAGINATION POWER: The participants will be blindfolded and will undergo a round in which they will be identifying the objects with the sense of touch placed in front of them.

8. FUN2SH: This event will be testing the level of the sense of humor the participant is having. Fun2sh will be having 4 rounds to showcase one’s funny side. The winner will be the one having the highest quotient of humor.
